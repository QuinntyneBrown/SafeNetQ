# L1 Requirements — SafeNetQ Community Emergency Mutual Aid Platform

## L1-1: User Authentication & KYC Onboarding
The platform shall provide secure user registration, authentication (with MFA), and FINTRAC-compliant KYC identity verification for all members.

## L1-2: Member Contribution Management
The platform shall enable members to set up, manage, and track monthly contributions via PAD, credit card, or Interac, with configurable tiers and transparent fee breakdowns.

## L1-3: Assistance Request & Review Workflow
The platform shall allow eligible members to submit emergency assistance requests with documentation, and provide a committee-based review, voting, and appeals process.

## L1-4: Payout Processing
The platform shall process approved payouts via direct deposit or Interac e-Transfer within 48-72 hours, enforcing per-event and annual limits.

## L1-5: Member Dashboard
The platform shall provide members with a dashboard showing contribution history, payout history, request status tracking, membership standing, and fund health indicators.

## L1-6: Community Feed & Notifications
The platform shall display an anonymized community feed of assistance events and deliver push/email notifications for contribution receipts, request updates, and announcements.

## L1-7: Document Vault
The platform shall provide secure, encrypted storage for member-uploaded verification documents with time-limited access controls.

## L1-8: Admin & Governance Dashboard
The platform shall provide admin interfaces for committee review, fund health monitoring, member management, AML/KYC management, audit trails, and RBAC.

## L1-9: Fund Financial Governance
The platform shall enforce fund architecture rules including trust/operating/reserve account separation, contribution splits, waiting periods, and payout caps.

## L1-10: Compliance & Reporting
The platform shall support FINTRAC reporting (STRs, large transaction reports), PIPEDA privacy compliance, audit logging, and generate monthly/annual financial reports.

## L1-11: Accessibility & Multi-Language Support
The platform shall meet WCAG 2.0 Level AA (AODA compliance) and support English, French, and additional community languages.

## L1-12: Responsive UI Design
The platform shall provide a fully responsive design across x-small, small, medium, large, and x-large screen sizes for all screens, including buttons, dialogs, toasts, and interactive components.

## L1-13: Mobile Application
The platform shall provide a native mobile application for iOS and Android with feature parity to the web application for member-facing features.

## L1-14: Backend API & Infrastructure
The platform shall provide a .NET 8+ backend API with Clean Architecture, PostgreSQL database, and secure hosting in Canadian data centers (Azure Canada Central or AWS ca-central-1).

## L1-15: Integration Services
The platform shall integrate with payment processors (Stripe/Moneris), identity verification (Jumio/Onfido), banking APIs (Flinks), email/SMS (SendGrid/Twilio), and encrypted document storage (AWS S3/Azure Blob in Canadian region).
